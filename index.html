What is the story behind Glass Half Full?

Over 844 million people are forced to live without safe water. We aim to promote awareness and educate people about this issue. We donate 20% of our profits to _______, a charity that addresses water sanitation issues. We hope that with your help, one day everyone will have access to a full glass of water.

What We Do

Using upcycled wood taken from the North Shore Transfer Station, the largest recycling station in North America under the umbrella of Emterra, we make hand-made coasters. Each coaster comes with a design of our water droplet mascot, Faroos, and a fact about the water and sanitation issues in our world. With our coasters, we hope to spread awareness and make a positive impact on our environment.

Who We Are

We are a group of five aspiring entrepreneurs who want to make a difference in our society. Our company, Glass Half Full, aims to reduce the size of our landfills by turning waste into a sustainable product. At the same time, our goal is to educate society about the social issues revolving water and sanitation.
